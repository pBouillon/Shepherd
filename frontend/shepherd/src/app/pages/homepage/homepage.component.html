Shepherd

Trustworthy information for the people, guarded by the people.

Shepherd is a collaborative tool that aims to bring together a community of people willing to promote trustworthy and objective information.

How does it work?

Shepherd heavily relies on its involved community through the dedicated browser extension.

Once downloaded, the extension will be activated whenever you will be browsing any media's website.

Thanks to it and the ratings provided by other members, a roughly estimate of how credible the media is will be displayed, helping you to stay alert regarding fake news and suspicious sources.

Contributing

Anybody can help Shepherd to be more accurate and trustworthy.

Using the extension, you are free to vote whether the media you are viewing can be trusted or not.

Using the extension, website, you can suggest any media that is not already reviewed by the community.

Privacy

Privacy matters, especially when it comes to public information and knowledge.

No opinion or content should be censored. That's why we aren't storing any personal information that might identify anyone. This way, you can freely give your insights without fearing any consequences from any group.
